Catch Three RGB

A simple endless colour matching/mixing game.

The player’s rolling cube starts out as one of the three base colours, Red, Green, and Blue, they must then collect one each of the other two before their cube “resets”.
As the falling coloured spheres are collected, the player’s colour changes accordingly, giving the player a coloured hint as to which colour is needed next… and which will end their run.

Game engine: Unity
Language: C#
Platform: WebGL
Other: Git, Blender, Gimp
