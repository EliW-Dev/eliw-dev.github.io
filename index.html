Well hello there, and welcome!


My name is Eli Whitbread, a game developer and programmer from Melbourne Australia.


I discovered the Unity game engine back in 2012, learning as much as I could, building prototypes and releasing 2 mobile games on Android, and a PC game on itch.io. Since 2021 though Unreal has become my game engine of choice; I do prefer working in C++ in Unreal but am comfortable with Blueprints as well.


I have worked professionally as a Gameplay Programmer since 2022, helping develop a wonderful 3rd person multiplayer game called Town Squared (Playlunch Games), using Unreal/C++.
